Recipe of Veg Momo

The ingrediants need's to make veg momo are


  • All purpose flour/ maida – 1 1/2 cup

  • Spring onion – 2 small size

  • Cabbage – 1 cup Chopped or grated

  • Capsicum – 1 (chopped )

  • Carrot – 1 grated

  • Ginger Garlic – 2 tsp chopped

  • Soya sauce – 1 tsp

  • Black pepper – 1/2 tsp

  • Oil

  • Salt

  • Coriander Leaves

Preparation of Veg Momo step by step

  1. Step-1 :

    • Mix flour, salt and oil in a bowl and add water to make a smooth dough.
      Just like our poori dough. Keep aside covered.


  1. Step-2:

    • Heat a pan with oil, add garlic, ginger.finely chopped white part of the spring onion and
      Add the chopped veggies and fry in high flame for a minute.


  1. Step-3:

    • Add salt,pepper, soya sauce and mix well. Continue to stir fry on high heat.


  1. Step-4:

    • Add 1 to 2 tbsp of the spring onions greens and stir fry for a minute.
      (check the taste and add more salt, pepper or soy sauce, if required).
      Let the stuffing cool completely.


  1. Step-5:

    • Now shaping the veg momos: Divide the dough into equal portions.Roll in dry flour
      and roll into small puris keeping the edges thin and the centre thick. Which is shown below.


  1. Step-6:

    • Place a portion of stuffing in the centre, fold one end over the other and give it a half moon shape.
      For another gather the edges and seal together. Do Steps as show below pics.


  1. Step-7:

    • Join all the pleats in the center. A pleated and shaped momos. Do Steps shown below pics.


  1. Step-8:

    • Prepare all momos this way and
      keep them covered under a moist napkin. till you are ready to steam them


  1. Step-9:

    • Use idli pans or pressure cooker to steam.Steam the veg momos for 10 minutes
      till the outer cover becomes transparent. (when you touch the momos then dough
      should not feel sticky to you. this means they are done and the momos will have a transparent look.)


  1. Step-10:

    • Serve Veg Momos with Chutney/sauce Enjoy !!!
